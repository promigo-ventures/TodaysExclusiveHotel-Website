ABOUT US

The Most Preferred Hotel in Alimosho

Today's Exclusive Hotel is the most preferred hotel in West Africa, and it is all about the right mix! Located in the heart of Victoria Island, we offer our clients a perfect blend of business & leisure amenities with dining and recreational options delicately infused in one amazing space. We crown all these with services that meet the highest international standards.

Overlooking the new Today's Exclusive Hotel, it is just a 10-minute drive to the City Centre and only 20minutes away from the Airport.

Our hotels are designed for your comfort and convenience. Your security is our primary concern and you will find our customer care second to none.
